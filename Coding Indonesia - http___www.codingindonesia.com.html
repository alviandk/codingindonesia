CODING & YOU

WHY CODING

Learn how to write computer program or "coding" give you more than just the core skills of a programmer, but it also teach you many others valuable skills such as analytical thinking, problem solving skill, sharpen your math skills, entrepreneurship, team work. All the skills that can be applied in may different domains.

WHO CAN BE A PROGRAMMER

Anyone can be a programmer or develop applications. It is not the privilege of a few people graduated from computer science school. You can also start as early as possible or even if you are already in a different profession, learning how to code will give you a big opportunity to increase your professional skills or open a new way as an entrepreneur. Children at age 7 can learn how to be a creator of games or applications.
